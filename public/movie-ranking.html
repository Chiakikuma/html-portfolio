My Top 3 Movies of all time

Parasite

Its a psychological thriller about class divisions and poverty. Despite its themes, its no documentary and is both stylistically beautiful and entertaining. Its an international film worth celebrating in the states.
PS. I dont remember much more than that but its an amazing experience to go into blind!

A Silent Voice

Another non-American film haha. This one has psychological elements as well but in a more grounded way. Its a touching story involving themes of alienation, depression, and suicidal ideation. It resonated with me in my darkest moments and made me feel understood

Coraline

Finally something from my country, but wait- it's animation! I usually prefer animation over live action because of the expressiveness and artistry. Coraline exemplifies this, despite being a children's movie its super creepy and features beautiful stop motion animation. I loved it so much as a child, I read the book as an adult and was not disappointed.
